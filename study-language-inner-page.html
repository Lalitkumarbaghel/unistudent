Study English in United Kingdom (UK)

Studying languages at the present time has many advantages, including that the foreign language is the most effective way to communicate and interact with others and make friends from different cultures, and it also creates a lot of job opportunities in various fields such as education, tourism, travel and other vital fields.

Learning a new language is a challenge for students before starting their studies, as the student must achieve a specific language level required by the university or college before engaging in the study.

The importance of learning and studying languages

Learning a second language besides the mother tongue is very important in a globalized world, some of these benefits are for example

  1. Studying a foreign language improves your chances of getting a job. Foreign languages provide a competitive advantage in career choices as one is able to communicate in a second language.
  2. International travel becomes easier and more enjoyable with knowledge of a foreign language.
  3. Analytical skills such as problem-solving and dealing with abstract concepts increase when you study a foreign language
  4. Multilingual people are helped by their ability to think in different languages and the ability to communicate and build academic and professional relationships in more than one language.
  5. Studying foreign languages enhances listening and memory skills, as the more you use your brain to learn new skills, the more your brain functions in recognizing new grammar and vocabulary.
  6. Studying a foreign language helps you to better understand your own language and culture.
  7. Knowing other languages encourages an appreciation of other cultures.

Conditions for learning languages in universities

1. IELTS test

It is the internationally accredited international English language test system that helps to work, study or migrate to a country where English is the mother tongue. This test works in countries such as Australia, Canada, Britain and America.

Through the test, your abilities and skills in listening, reading, writing and speaking English will be assessed during the test on a scale of 1-9.

The aim is to help you if you are looking to work, live or study in an English-speaking country, you must be able to demonstrate a high level of English language ability.

2. TOEFL test

The Test of English as a Foreign Language is a standardised test to measure the English language ability of non-native speakers who wish to enrol in English-speaking universities.

Those who take the TOEFL test usually want to prove English language proficiency for an academic purpose in order to gain entry to a university or graduate study abroad, a foreign high school, exchange program, community college, or to obtain a student visa.

Pre-Sessional English Courses

Students who do not meet the English language entry requirements at a university or college can take an introductory course to develop their language skills before starting studies. These courses improve your level of English to prepare for your undergraduate or postgraduate degree.

At the end of the course, students who reach the required level of English can begin their studies. A short program of remedial language study is offered to improve the skills of those who have not succeeded. After that, students who reach the required level of English after the remedial course can begin their studies. Those who do not pass the catch-up course will be deferred to the next language study programme.

Customised language tests within the university

It is a test that students take to determine their language level before enrolling in the study at the university or college. It is usually exposed to foreign students or those whose secondary studies were in a language different from the language of the university or the country in which they will complete their university studies.
