UAE Scholarship

here are many options of external scholarship for academically distinguished students in the UAE to pursue their undergraduate and postgraduate studies in many internationally recognized universities abroad. The state also provides all means and facilities for scholarships and students to enroll and study in one of its universities or other international universities abroad.

Scholarships of the Ministry of Education

Students can apply to the Ministry of Education for external scholarships, in this article we will focus on how to go through all the steps to apply for a scholarship from the Ministry of Education to study outside the UAE.

Application procedures for scholarships of the Ministry of Education in the UAE

The official website of the Ministry of Education offers a range of services to facilitate students’ access to everything they need, the most important of which is the “Scholarship Application” service, which allows students to apply for scholarships to complete their degrees abroad at the Ministry of Education’s expense.

Here is the procedure for applying for scholarships in the UAE to study abroad:
  1. Submit an application through the Ministry's website and complete all required data, including general information, contact information, previous eligibility data, and required scholarships.
  2. Upload all documents required for foreign scholarships.

The staff in the External Scholarships

  1. Department receives and reviews the application, then works to ensure all required information and documentation.
  2. Emirates Scholarships Office staff checks the conditions and criteria in the application.
  3. The Emirates Scholarships Office staff prepares a checklist for all scholarship candidates.
  4. The Director of Scholarships presents all nominated applications to the Committee for a decision of approval or rejection.
  5. The scholarship officer will contact the nominated students to arrange interviews.
  6. Submitting a ministerial decision regarding students to the Minister of Education for approval.
  7. Contacting students to complete the task implementation process and coordinating with national embassies and cultural offices to inform them of scholarships.

Requirements and conditions for scholarships for undergraduate students

These requirements are divided into two categories, students with a high school diploma and students with an advanced diploma.

1. High school graduates
  1. Fulfilment of general conditions
  2. A general secondary certificate or its equivalent, with conditions determined by the Ministry of Education.
  3. Obtaining a high school diploma or its equivalent in a science course to study engineering, medicine, life sciences, and natural sciences.
  4. Scores required for each subject on the UAEU Admission Standard Test or EmSAT.
  5. Studying with total regularity in the country of study.
  6. The age of the students is 17 years and over.

  8. The scholarship does not exceed two years after the student graduates from high school or its equivalent.

Abu Dhabi Oil Company Scholarships for Distribution (ADNOC)

The ADNOC Scholarship Program is a prestigious program awarded to a select number of talented students following a highly competitive application process.

Founded in 1974, ADNOC continues to nurture the next generation of UAE nationals by investing in their higher education.

This fund has been developed to enable high-potential Emirati citizens to be trained in various technical disciplines, as the program is being implemented in line with the vision of advancing the industrial and economic goals of ADNOC and the UAE.

What does the ADNOC Scholarship Program offer to accepted students

The program provides guidance and support for outstanding students to pursue undergraduate and postgraduate programs in specialised disciplines in reputable institutions, both within the United Arab Emirates and abroad.

Successful applicants to the ADNOC Scholarship Program can expect to benefit from the following
  1. Scholarship application assistance
  2. Tuition fee support
  3. Access the ADNOC Scholarship Portal Rewards for excellent performance
  4. Support for living expenses
  5. The ADNOC Scholarships Universities list includes more than 80 of the most prestigious institutions in the world, according to the Times Higher Education rankings of the top 100 global universities.

Scholarships of the Telecommunications Regulatory Authority and Digital Government:

The Communications Regulatory Authority and the Communications and Information Technology Development Fund of the digital government launched an important plan to advance the educational process by supporting and developing scientifically distinguished national cadres, in a way that enhances the role and position of the national education sector in related disciplines in the field of communications and information technology.

Fund has invested heavily in an important initiative to allocate hundreds of academic seats for bachelor's and master's degrees to prestigious universities in the United Arab Emirates, as well as international universities in the United States, Japan, Australia and the United Kingdom.

The program aims to attract 1,000 students to the scholarship program over the next few years.

The program is part of the fund's strategy to raise the level of talent in the fields of communications and information technology, including computer engineering, electrical engineering, computer science, information technology, and many other disciplines.
